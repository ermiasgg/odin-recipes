Kekel

Kekel is also one of Ethipians favorite dish,

it is a lamb soup with different condiment.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • A lamb shank and meat
  • Oil
  • butter
  • Hashed onion
  • Salt
  • condiments

Steps

  1. Putting the plate to fire
  2. Adding the water
  3. Adding the lamb shank and meat
  4. Adding the oil
  5. Adiing the hashed onion
  6. Waiting them ripen
  7. Adding salt and condiments
  8. Adding the butter
