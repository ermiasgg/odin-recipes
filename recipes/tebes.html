Tebes

Tebes is one of Ethipians favorite dish,

which is a roasted meat with different condiment.

Ingredients

  • Hashed meat
  • Oil
  • Hashed onion
  • Salt
  • Cultural condiment

Steps

  1. Warming the pan
  2. Adding oil to the warmed pan
  3. Adding the hashed meat
  4. Adiing the hashed onion
  5. Waiting them ripen
  6. Adding salt and condiments
